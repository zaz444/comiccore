Roles & Responsibilities

What Moderators Can Do

  • Review reports submitted by users for content that violates the Terms of Service.
  • Remove content that is harmful, inappropriate, or infringing.
  • Issue warnings or temporary restrictions to accounts in violation.
  • Escalate serious matters to the platform owner (DDG / @jeffyplays).
  • Assist with DMCA takedown requests and copyright claims.
  • Support users with platform-related questions and issues.

⚠️ Moderators will never ask for your password. If someone claiming to be a moderator requests your password, report it immediately.

Appeals

Appealing a Moderation Decision

If you believe a moderation action taken against your account or content was made in error, you may appeal. To do so:

  • Contact a moderator or the platform owner through the platform's messaging system or by email at lilpayd6@gmail.com.
  • Clearly explain why you believe the decision was incorrect.
  • Appeals are reviewed by the owner (DDG / @jeffyplays) and a final decision will be issued.

Abusive or repeat appeals may result in further enforcement action. All final decisions rest with the platform owner.

Reporting

How to Report Content or a User

If you encounter content or behavior that violates our community standards, you can report it directly within the platform. Reports are reviewed by the moderation team as soon as possible. All reports are treated confidentially.

How We Use Your Data

A plain-language breakdown of exactly what data ComicCore collects, why we collect it, and how it's used — no legal jargon.

Effective: April 6, 2026 Last Updated: April 6, 2026
Overview

Our Data Philosophy

We believe in being upfront. ComicCore only collects data that is genuinely necessary to run the platform, keep it safe, and improve your experience. We do not sell your data. We do not use it for advertising. We do not train AI systems with your content.

✅ Simple rule: Your data is used to run ComicCore. Nothing more.

Section 1

Data We Collect & Why

Data Type Why We Collect It Stored?
Username & Email To create and manage your account, and for login/authentication. Yes
Profile Picture & Bio To display your public profile to other users. Yes
Comics & Panels To publish your creations and make them viewable by others. Yes
Uploaded Sprites To add characters to the community sprite gallery. Yes
Squad Messages & Comments To enable real-time communication between users. Yes
Theme & Settings To remember your display preferences between sessions. Local only
Login Session (Cookie) To keep you logged in securely without re-authenticating. Session
IP Address For security, fraud prevention, and abuse detection. Temporarily
Device / Browser Info To display the platform correctly on your device. Temporarily
Usage Analytics To understand how features are used and improve the platform. Aggregated
Section 2

What We Never Do With Your Data

  • ❌ Never sell your personal data to third parties or data brokers.
  • ❌ Never use your content or data to train AI or machine learning models.
  • ❌ Never show you targeted or behavioral advertisements.
  • ❌ Never share your private messages with other users or third parties except as required by law.
  • ❌ Never retain data longer than necessary once your account is deleted.
Section 3

Who Can See Your Content

Depending on your account settings and the content type:

Content Visible To
Published Comics All ComicCore users (public by default)
Your Profile All users who visit your profile
Squad Messages Members of your squad only
Sprite Gallery Submissions All users once approved
Your Email ComicCore team only (never public)
Account Settings You only
Section 4

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account:

  • Your profile, comics, and personal data are removed within a reasonable period.
  • Some data may be retained in anonymized, aggregated form for analytics.
  • Content in squad chats may remain visible in a deleted/anonymous state to preserve conversation context for other members.
  • Backups may retain data for a limited additional period before being purged.
Section 5

Moderator Data Access

ComicCore moderators may access certain account information strictly for the purposes of:

  • Investigating reported content or behavior that violates our Terms of Service.
  • Responding to DMCA takedown requests.
  • Verifying user identity in the event of an account dispute.
  • Maintaining platform safety and removing harmful content.

Moderators are bound by confidentiality expectations and may not use their access for personal purposes. See the Team & Moderation page for more about our moderators.

⚠️ Moderators do not have access to your password. Passwords are never stored in plain text.

Section 6

Your Data Controls

You can manage your data directly through your account settings. Options include editing your profile, deleting your comics, leaving squads, and deleting your account entirely. For specific data requests, contact a moderator, the platform owner, or email us at lilpayd6@gmail.com.

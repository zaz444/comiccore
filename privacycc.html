Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data ComicCore collects, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

Effective: April 6, 2026 Last Updated: April 6, 2026
Section 1

Overview

ComicCore™ ("we," "us," "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use the ComicCore platform, and explains your rights regarding your data.

📌 We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Full stop.

Section 2

Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

Information You Provide:

  • Account information (username, email address, profile picture, bio).
  • Content you create and publish (comics, panels, captions).
  • Sprites and characters you upload or submit to the gallery.
  • Messages and communications within the platform (squad chats, comments).
  • Preferences and settings you configure.

Information Collected Automatically:

  • Usage data (pages visited, features used, time spent).
  • Device information (browser type, operating system, screen size).
  • IP address and approximate geographic location.
  • Cookies and local storage data for session management and preferences.
Section 3

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the ComicCore platform.
  • Authenticate your identity and manage your account.
  • Display your comics and profile to other users (based on your privacy settings).
  • Send important service notifications and updates.
  • Respond to support requests and moderate content.
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and violations of our Terms.
  • Analyze usage trends to improve platform features.

We do not use your data to train AI/ML models, serve advertisements, or sell to data brokers.

Section 4

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard practices. We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

⚠️ ComicCore may store data on third-party cloud infrastructure. By using our platform, you acknowledge this.

Section 5

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who help operate the platform (e.g., hosting, authentication), bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of users or the public.
  • Business Transfer: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user data may be transferred as part of that transaction.
Section 6

Cookies & Local Storage

ComicCore uses cookies and browser local storage to remember your login session, theme preferences, and other settings. These are essential for the platform to function properly.

You may disable cookies in your browser settings, but doing so may prevent some features from working correctly.

Section 7

Your Rights

As a ComicCore user, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

👁️

Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

✏️

Correction

Update or correct inaccurate information in your profile.

🗑️

Deletion

Request deletion of your account and associated data.

🚫

Objection

Object to certain uses of your data where permitted by law.

To exercise these rights, contact us through the platform's support channels or by email at lilpayd6@gmail.com.

Section 8

Children's Privacy

ComicCore is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at lilpayd6@gmail.com and we will take steps to delete such information.

Section 9

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the platform or email. Continued use of ComicCore after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

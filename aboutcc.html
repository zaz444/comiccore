What Is ComicCore

About the Platform

ComicCore™ is a web-based comic creation platform that makes storytelling accessible to everyone. Instead of requiring drawing skills, ComicCore lets you build comics using sprites — pre-made 2D characters you can pose, layer, and arrange into panels to tell any story you imagine.

Whether you're a lifelong fan of retro games, an aspiring comic artist, or just someone with a story to tell, ComicCore gives you the tools to bring it to life.

About the Sprite Gallery

The ComicCore sprite gallery is a growing library of characters available to use in your comics. It includes:

What Is ComicCore?

About the App

ComicCore is a web based comic platform that makes stories from comics or novels accessible to everyone. Instead of only drawing skills, ComicCore lets you make comics using sprites as well which are 2D characters that have actions. You can give them a dialogue, a background, and make your own creative story.

Whether you like video games, love making comics, love superheroes, or just writing, ComicCore lets you do it all.

Features

What You Can Do

Create Comics

Build your multi-frame stories using sprites, backgrounds, effects, text, and more.

Sprite Gallery

Browse community-submitted sprites uploaded by other ComicCore creators to use in your comics.

Squads

Join or create squads to co-create comics, share your work, and chat with other creators on ComicCore.

Read & Discover

Explore comics made by the community and read the best stories.

Favorites

Save comics and creators you love for easy access later.

Themes

Switch between color swatches in settings for your preferred look.

About the Sprite Gallery

The ComicCore sprite gallery is a growing collection of characters available to use in your comics. It includes:

ComicCore Originals & Public Domain: Characters that are designed specifically for the platform or sourced from the public domain. These are free to use by all users.

Community Submissions: Sprites uploaded by users, which may include characters from games, drawings, and other media. These are submitted and used at the community's discretion. See our Copyright Policy for how this works legally.

Community Guidelines

ComicCore is built for creativity and fun. These guidelines help make sure it stays that way for everyone.

The Spirit

What We're About

ComicCore is a place to be creative, share stories, and connect with others who love comics and characters. We want it to be welcoming, imaginative, and safe — for beginners and veterans alike.

These guidelines aren't meant to be restrictive — they exist to protect the community and keep ComicCore a place people love coming back to.

Do's

What We Encourage

🎨

Be Creative

Experiment, try new styles, tell weird stories, and make things nobody's made before. That's what ComicCore is for.

🤝

Be Kind & Respectful

Treat other creators with respect. Feedback is welcome — cruelty is not. Build others up.

📢

Report Violations

If you see something that breaks these guidelines, report it. You're helping protect the whole community.

👏

Credit Inspiration

If your work is heavily inspired by another creator's style or story, give them a shoutout. It's just good vibes.

🌍

Be Inclusive

ComicCore welcomes creators of all backgrounds, skill levels, and interests. Make sure your interactions reflect that.

Don'ts

What's Not Allowed

🚫

No Hate or Harassment

No slurs, targeted harassment, threats, or discriminatory content based on race, gender, religion, sexuality, disability, or any other identity.

🔞

No Explicit Adult Content

No sexual or graphic violent content. Keep it appropriate for a general audience.

🤡

No Impersonation

Don't pretend to be another user, moderator, the owner, or official ComicCore staff.

📣

No Spam

Don't flood feeds, comment sections, or squad chats with repetitive or irrelevant content.

⚠️

No Ban Evasion

Don't create new accounts to get around a ban or suspension. This will result in all associated accounts being removed.

🔗

No Malicious Links

Don't post phishing links, scams, or links to harmful content anywhere on the platform.

⚠️ Violating these guidelines may result in content removal, warnings, temporary suspension, or a permanent ban — depending on severity.

Enforcement

How We Handle Violations

Our moderation team reviews reported content and takes action based on the severity and context of each violation. Actions range from a warning to a permanent ban. Decisions can be appealed — see the Team page for the appeals process.

We believe in proportional enforcement. First-time, minor mistakes are usually handled with a warning. Repeated or severe violations are taken more seriously.

✅ Our goal is to protect the community — not to punish people for honest mistakes.

Final Note

These Guidelines May Evolve

As ComicCore grows, these guidelines will be updated to reflect new situations and community needs. We'll always announce major changes. Thanks for being part of making ComicCore a great place.

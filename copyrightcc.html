Copyright Policy

ComicCore respects intellectual property rights and has a clear framework for how copyrighted characters and content are handled on the platform.

Effective: April 6, 2026 Last Updated: April 6, 2026
Section 1

Overview

ComicCore™ is a creative platform that allows users to make comics using sprites and characters. Our platform contains two distinct types of character content, each governed differently under copyright law.

ComicCore takes copyright seriously and operates in good faith in accordance with applicable law, including the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) and relevant international copyright frameworks.

Section 2

Types of Character Content

✅ Platform-Provided

Public Domain & Original Characters

Characters drawn by ComicCore's artists or sourced from the public domain. These are freely usable within the platform with no copyright concerns.

👤 User-Submitted

User-Uploaded Sprites

Characters submitted by users, which may include third-party copyrighted characters. ComicCore does not generate or distribute these independently.

📌 ComicCore acts as a neutral platform host for user-submitted content — similar to how YouTube or Reddit hosts user videos and posts. We are not the source of third-party copyrighted characters.

Section 3

Legal Basis for User-Submitted Copyrighted Content

ComicCore's ability to host user-submitted content that may include copyrighted characters rests on established legal principles:

DMCA Safe Harbor (17 U.S.C. § 512): ComicCore qualifies as an online service provider under the DMCA. This means we are not liable for infringing content uploaded by users, provided we:

  • Have no actual knowledge of infringing content.
  • Do not financially benefit directly from infringement.
  • Promptly remove infringing content upon receiving valid DMCA notices.
  • Have a designated DMCA agent and a repeat infringer policy.

User Responsibility: Users who submit copyrighted characters bear sole legal responsibility for that content. By uploading copyrighted material, a user represents that they have the rights or authorization to share that content on the platform. ComicCore does not verify ownership of user-submitted sprites.

⚠️ If you are a rights holder and believe your work has been posted without authorization, please use our DMCA process below to request removal.

Fan Art & Fair Use Considerations: Some user-submitted sprites may constitute fan art, transformative works, or parody. While these may qualify for fair use protections in some jurisdictions, ComicCore cannot provide legal advice on what constitutes fair use in any individual case. Rights holders retain the right to assert their intellectual property regardless.

Section 5

DMCA Takedown Process

If you are a copyright owner or authorized agent who believes that content on ComicCore infringes your copyright, you may submit a DMCA takedown notice. Here's how:

1

Identify the Content

Locate the specific sprite, comic, or content you believe infringes your copyright, and note its URL or location within the platform.

2

Prepare Your Notice

Your notice must include: identification of the copyrighted work, identification of the infringing material and its location, your contact information, a statement of good faith belief, a statement of accuracy under penalty of perjury, and your signature.

3

Submit to ComicCore

Send your DMCA notice to ComicCore's moderation team through the platform's official support channels or directly by email at lilpayd6@gmail.com.

4

We Review & Respond

We will review your notice and, if valid, promptly remove or disable access to the identified content. We will notify the uploader of the action taken.

⚖️ Filing a false DMCA claim may expose you to legal liability under 17 U.S.C. § 512(f). Please only file if you are the legitimate rights holder or authorized agent.

Section 6

Counter-Notices

If you believe your content was removed in error, you may submit a counter-notice. A valid counter-notice must include your identification, identification of the removed content, a statement under penalty of perjury that the removal was made in error or misidentification, and your consent to jurisdiction of the relevant court.

Upon receiving a valid counter-notice, we may restore the content after a statutory waiting period unless the original complainant files a court action.

Section 7

Repeat Infringer Policy

ComicCore has a strict repeat infringer policy. Users who repeatedly upload infringing content will have their accounts suspended or permanently terminated, in accordance with DMCA safe harbor requirements.
